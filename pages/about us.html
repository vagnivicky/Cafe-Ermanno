About Us

Our coffee has both Italian and French origin, located in the center of Copenhagen, Denmark. but this does not have any Danish touch since when the idea of creating the cafe was thought of, we wanted to merge the two cultural visions that most attracted us. You can see in our cafe that there is a portrait of a well-known actress of Italian origin Sofia Loren. As well as the origin of our coffee comes from the same area. The French rustic style, the decorations on the copper pots, the cheese plate. This is how we tried to bring the best version of both cultures in one cafe.
